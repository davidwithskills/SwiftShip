Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services.

We currently service all 50 states in the US and over 50 countries worldwide. Our international network is constantly expanding. You can check service availability by entering your destination zip code during the booking process.

You can track your package using the tracking number provided in your confirmation email. Simply enter this number in the tracking section of our website or mobile app for real-time updates on your shipment's location and estimated delivery time.

We prohibit shipping hazardous materials, illegal substances, firearms, ammunition, perishable goods (without our temperature-controlled service), live animals, and cash. For a complete list of prohibited items, please refer to our Terms of Service.

If we fail to meet our guaranteed delivery time for express services, you may be eligible for a full or partial refund of your shipping costs. Refund requests must be submitted within 14 days of the delivery date. Please note that refunds are not available for standard shipping services.

You can schedule a pickup through our website or mobile app after creating a shipment. Pickups are available Monday through Friday from 8am to 6pm, and Saturdays from 9am to 2pm in most areas. Same-day pickup may be available if scheduled before 12pm local time.
